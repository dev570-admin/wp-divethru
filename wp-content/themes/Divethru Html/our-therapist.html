Searching for the right therapist for you?

We’re making that process easier with our DiveThru Therapists.

Match with a Therapist
Connecting with your therapist is important for successful outcomes so we make sure our DiveThru therapists reflect a range of identities, approaches and lived experiences. We’re intentional about matching you with the best therapist for you so that you feel seen, heard and understood.

The DiveThru
Therapy Values

There’s a reason why we work with the therapists that we do! Well, at least five reasons to be precise. The DiveThru Studio is a safe and welcoming space—and we achieve that with these values.
  • Inclusivity
  • Variety in Areas of Focus & Specialties
  • Cultural Sensitivity
  • Diverse Backgrounds & Experiences
  • Compassion & Empathy
  • Variety in Therapeutic Approaches

Find Your Therapist Match

Struggling with your mental health but don’t know where to start? Overwhelmed by how much time and effort it takes to search for a therapist? Take our quick matching questionnaire! You’ll get your top 3 DiveThru Therapist matches and from there, booking your first session is a breeze.
