Meet your new support system

You’re not alone in your struggles so it’s time to stop struggling alone.
Here’s how we can help you DiveThru whatever you’re going through:
A Space for Therapy

At DiveThru, you can find a therapist who gets you. Access one-on-one, group, couples or family therapy at our studio (coming in January), in our app or through a combination of both.

A Space for Community

We’re not alone in our struggles so it’s time to stop struggling alone. You can find a community of people who get you through our app, in-person at our studios (coming January), in- person groups, workshops and event offerings.

A Space for You

At DiveThru, we’re focused on creating a safe space for YOU to work on your mental health. Whether you’re showing up at our DiveThru Studio or connecting with our therapist-created resources in app, we have something to help you DiveThru whatever you’re going thru.
