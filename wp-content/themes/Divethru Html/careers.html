Work With Us

And help young people take charge of their mental health.

Hi! We’re DiveThru

Our mission is to ensure no young person struggles alone. We’re making that happen through one-on-one therapy, group support and self-guided digital tools, delivered virtually and in person.

What We Value

Community & Belonging

At DiveThru, we’re creating a safe, welcoming, and inclusive space where young people can access mental health support and connect with a community of people who really truly understand what it means to struggle with mental health.

We Get It

We’ve been there and we know what it’s like to feel so alone in your struggles. As our founder likes to say, “It fucking sucks feeling unsafe in your own head.” That’s how DiveThru started and that intimate understanding of mental health is woven throughout every service we provide.

Human-Centred Care

Young people’s needs and wants matter. You matter. Mental healthcare at DiveThru starts with the unique humans who visit us! Everything from the physical studio space to the therapy session structure is developed with their needs in mind.

Why Work For Us

You’ll be joining a team that is dedicated to reimagining access to mental heath support. We think that’s cool AF but we’re obvs biased! So here is a list of other perks!

  • Competitive salary
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Safe & inclusive studio space
  • Company culture that prioritizes mental health
  • Opportunity to host workshops and community events
  • Opportunity to host sessions in person, online or hybrid
  • Winter break: Christmas to New Years
  • Opportunities to grow professionally
